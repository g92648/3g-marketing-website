Frequently Asked Questions

What is SAP Business One phase-2 consulting?

Phase-2 consulting is optimization work on a live SAP B1 system after go-live. It covers configuration gaps, broken workflows, missing reports, integrations, and AI automation - the work the original partner didn't finish or didn't do right.

Can you fix a bad SAP Business One implementation?

Yes. We audit the original configuration against your actual business processes and deliver a prioritized remediation plan. Most clients see immediate wins within the first two weeks.

How long does SAP B1 optimization take?

Emergency fixes resolve in days. A full phase-2 audit takes 4-8 weeks. Ongoing optimization and integration work runs on a monthly retainer. We scope everything upfront with a technical audit before any commitment.

What industries do you serve?

Do you work with SAP partners?
